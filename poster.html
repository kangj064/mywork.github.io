Personal Artwork

09.2023 - 10. 2023

I designed a "Home" book inspired by the game of "Minesweeper." Minesweeper, resembling the shape of a house, shares a similarity where losing one space means losing the house. This symbolizes that losing a home signifies being unable to reach my true home, a memory of mine when I lose.

"If you lose even one, you lose your home."

Inspired by the game 'Hopscotch,' I divided my memories into parts, just like in Land Grab 1, 2, and 3. I aimed to give them a geometric aesthetic. All memories and recollections, whether negative or positive, are my own, and they are my home, shaping who I am.
