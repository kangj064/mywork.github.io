Asphalt Book

Book Design

Personal Artwork

09.2023 - 10. 2023

I focused on the locker paint for the construction on the asphalt in the city of New York. It may be easy to overlook, but it ultimately signifies that the city is working for us. By connecting invisible pipelines or gas lines through lines, I aimed to turn this into a book and emphasize the sense of community in our community. I created it as an unfoldable poster book by not binding it.

""The extension of invisible connecting lines.""
