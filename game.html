Personal Artwork

09.2023 - 10. 2023

This is an infinity runner mobile game that I designed and coded.
I created the game's visuals using traditional Korean folk paintings,
aiming to capture people's interest in the past.

"If you don't take an interest in the past, it will be forgotten."

This is the value system that I primarily focused on while creating this artwork. To truly understand the world, it's essential to have a grasp of history. Without knowledge of the past, we may repeat the same mistakes. Moreover, it's important to comprehend and emphasize our culture and values. That's why I created this game.

This game utilizes traditional Korean folk tales to convey history and culture in an engaging and accessible way. Within the game, various folk tales are intertwined, and players strive to uncover the true values and meaning hidden within them. Through this, I aim to promote an understanding of our unique values and heritage while delivering significant history and culture in a more thoughtful manner.
